# ThugNet Feature Requests

Want something added to ThugNet? Click the pencil icon on this file, add a
block above the `---` line below, and commit. That is the whole process.
Below that line are receipts showing what happened to past requests --
leave that part alone.

Put the title on the `##` line and anything else underneath it:

    ## Search box on the Domains page
    Scrolling is slow once you have 12 domains. — yourname

An agent picks it up within a few minutes and replaces your block with a
receipt line below the `---` rule, so you can see what happened to it.
